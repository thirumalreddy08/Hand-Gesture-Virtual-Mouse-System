About the Project

Aura Virtual Mouse uses advanced computer vision and MediaPipe hand tracking to replace physical interaction devices.

It reads spatial coordinates arrayed on your hand in real-time to translate analog movements into precise digital cursors, clicks, and system commands.

User Manual (Gestures)

  • V-Shape: Move Cursor
  • Fist: Hold Left Click (Drag Drop)
  • Middle Finger: Single Click
  • Two Fingers: Double Click
  • Pinch (Right Hand): Brightness/Volume
  • Pinch (Left Hand): Scrolling
